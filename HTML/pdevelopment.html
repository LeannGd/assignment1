Personal Development


Strengths

Being able to be objective

- I have learned how to detach myself from situations without taking situations personal.
I was able to develop myself in this area because I did CAPE Law.My teacher was a lawyer herself and activities and assignments
guided us to look at the bigger picture and note the facts.
Team Player

- I have been developing myself in this area in the recent years because I had to interact and complete tasks in groups or seek help from
others without the fear of being judged. Being a team player played a major role in the companies that I have
worked, as well as school. It is basically a requirement to be able to work in social spaces.
Easy going

- I have been learning to be more approachable and friendly. I have learned how to create a safe space for persons to talk comfortably. I also
have been learning how to not make a bad day that I am experiencing affect another person.


Summary

“Twelve Pillars” by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener touched on very important topics. The chapters that we were advised to read was of great importance
because it encouraged me to be serious about making goals and the road to achieve them. After reading those chapters I became inclined to tweak the goals I had written
down for the new year and make them more realistic and in order. The chapters highlighted that the journey and process is not as easy as it is portrayed, and it takes
dedication and discipline.The authors also added wise words which I made note of because sometimes us young adults we discouraged easily or procrastinatewithout realising that a result of our actions will push back our goals.
